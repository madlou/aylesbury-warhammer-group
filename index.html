Aylesbury Warhammer 40K & AoS Group

Background

The Facebook group was formed in August 2021 by a small group of 40K players looking for a new place to arrange games at each others homes, but quickly developed into a community that grew way beyond that.

Our goal now is to be a central hub for all Warhammer hobby/gaming related content for anyone living in and around Aylesbury!

Off the back of lockdown restrictions being lifted, there was a huge demand for us to create a regular meet-up and in November 2021 we secured an evening at a local community centre.

Other things to note:

  • We strongly recommend arranging games in advance and pre-booking a table - mainly to prevent disappointment.
  • We will always try our best to accommodate new players with an introduction game, to introduce them to the hobby. Please free to just turn up if you're new!
  • We will let you know any issues in the unlikely event we're completely full.
  • There is plenty of parking available.
  • We can fit around 6-7 tables in (12-14 players)
  • Its always free to come down and have a chat and look at whats going on, everyone is welcome.
  • Use our Facebook chats to find opponents and book your table.

Our Culture

  • Our group is for everyone and inclusivity is important to us
  • We are committed to growing the group and welcoming new players
  • We will encourage engagement through leagues, ladders and competitions
  • We encourage fun games over competitive
  • Tournament practice games are fine as long as you communicate this with your opponent
  • We value our relationship with the local Warhammer store

Our Rules

  1. Subs must be paid for on the day of game; cash or bank transfer
  2. Gaming hall to be left tidier than we found it
  3. Warhammer is for everyone
  4. Zero tolerance of any abusive actions
  5. Any kind of cheating not tolerated
  6. Unpainted allowed. Proxies allowed. Kitbashing allowed. Limited 3D printed models allowed.
  7. Games Workshop games only (40K, AoS, KT, Bloodbowl, LofR, etc)
  8. Light snacks only. No gresy food allowed.
  9. Official meet-ups are for over 18s only (may change in the future)
  10. Facebook Group is for over 13s (Facebook rule)
  11. Don't touch other peoples models without permission
  12. Everyone is subject to rules, including committee members
  13. Rules can only be changed with a majority committee vote

Annual General Meetings

The next AGM will be held on 8th January 2024.

  • Held every year in January
  • Anyone that has attended more than 5 meet-ups can attend, nominate and vote
  • Nominate yourself for committee roles, stating why you would be good at it
  • We all vote for who we want in role, highest vote wins, one person per role (draw decided by current Chairman)
  • Once committee members decided, let the party commence - first drink on the group!

Periodic Committee Meetings

  • Meet at an agreed number of times per year (every 3-6 months)
  • Agenda to be provided in advance by Secretary
  • Discuss what has worked well
  • Discuss what could be better
  • Vote on any rules changes
  • Vote on any initiatives
  • Have a beer and be merry!
